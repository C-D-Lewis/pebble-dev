# Skyarc

Daily weather at a glance - a watchface with a minimal design, with general weather conditions for the whole day shown around the outside of the main arc, and relative temperatures around the inside. Sunrise and sunset are also indicated.

If an hour is 'clear' weather, no arc segment is shown. Temperatures are shown relative to the min/max for the day - coldest is darkest blue with two extra gradations for 1 and 2 degrees above, and hottest is darkest red with a similar scheme of two ligher shades. Greys represent clouds. Black and white watches will see solid white for rain/snow/fog/storms etc,

When shaken, the outer weather arc is replaced with chance of precipitation. The thicker the segment, the higher chance of rain. Other current weather data is also shown, such as wind speed and humidity.

The battery level is shown in the middle bar, and is green/white when Bluetooth is connected or grey otherwise.

App configuration includes choice of temperature/wind speed units, background color, and disabling of animations.

Weather data from [OpenMeteo](https://open-meteo.com/).
